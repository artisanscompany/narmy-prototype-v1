Design Notes

  • Top nav (not sidebar) — personnel don't need 20 nav items, a clean horizontal bar keeps focus on their content
  • Status cards with color-coded top borders — gold for pay, green for active, army green for rank, amber for open issues
  • Quick actions in greeting row — "Download Payslip" and "Raise Complaint" are the two most common actions, always visible
  • Pay breakdown dark footer row — net pay in gold on dark green, visually anchors the most important number
  • Complaint card uses amber/warning style — draws attention to active issues without alarm
  • Monospace for all monetary values and IDs — precision, readability, military data aesthetic
  • AWOL variant — for Cpl. Bello, the greeting area gets a full-width red banner, status card turns red, and a restriction notice appears
